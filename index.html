Bio & Statement ↗

I am a South African fine artist working in acrylic and mixed media. Twenty years of professional creative practice — across design, art direction, and brand — have sharpened a painter's eye that finds the tension between the poetry and music of an image: reading with clarity and confidence from a distance, yet revealing bold, expressive brushstrokes and my unmistakable touch up close.

Drawing on subjects that range from the iconic to the unexpected, I bridge the worlds of fine art and popular culture, rendered with energy, intention, and a distinctly South African perspective.

For me, making art is therapy: a way of expressing yourself to find yourself. Faith, family, and creativity sit at the core of everything I make.

My work is represented on Saatchi Art and held in private collections across South Africa and abroad. Commissions and gallery enquiries are welcome.
