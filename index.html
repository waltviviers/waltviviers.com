Walt Viviers is a South African fine artist working in acrylic and mixed media. His practice explores the tension between the poetry and music of the artwork — images that read with clarity and confidence from a distance, yet reveal bold, expressive brushstrokes and the artist's unmistakable touch up close.

Drawing on subjects that range from the iconic to the unexpected, Viviers bridges the worlds of fine art and popular culture, rendered with energy, intention, and a distinctly South African perspective.

Works are held in private collections. Commissions and gallery enquiries are welcome.
