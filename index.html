Work that holds

Walt Viviers is a South African fine artist working in oil and mixed media. His practice explores the tension between mark and restraint — images that resist immediate resolution and reward sustained looking.

Works are held in private collections internationally. Commissions, studio visits, and gallery enquiries are welcome.
