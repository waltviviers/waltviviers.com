How to Play

  • Pick a mode from the start screen or menu
  • Dog: tap to walk · hold to scritch · tap ball or stick to throw · double-tap to dig
  • Rat: tap rat to scritch · tap ground to place cheese · rats breed freely · every 10th is a dumbo (big ears) · every 16th is a naked rat (pink) · every 15th cheese triggers a raid cat
  • Cat: hold to purr and wake · tap ground for laser · laser won't wake sleeping cats
  • Visitor animals arrive now and then
