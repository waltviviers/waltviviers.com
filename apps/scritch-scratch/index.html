What's in the yard

Your dog wanders the yard, digs up buried things, and fidgets when bored. Tap to scritch. Tap a ball or stick to throw it — watch your dog chase it down. A visitor dog may drop in, steal a bone, and disappear. Rats bolt when strangers arrive.
