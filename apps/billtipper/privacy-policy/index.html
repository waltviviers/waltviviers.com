Bill Tipper — App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026

Bill Tipper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All calculations happen entirely on your device.

What We Collect

Nothing. Bill Tipper is a simple tip and bill-splitting calculator. It requires no account, no sign-in, and no network connection to function. No information is collected from you at any time.

Data Storage

Bill Tipper does not store any data beyond the current session. When you close the app, all values are cleared. Nothing is written to external servers, analytics platforms, or third-party services.

Third-Party Services

Bill Tipper does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics tools, or crash-reporting services. There are no trackers of any kind.

Permissions

Bill Tipper requests no device permissions. It does not access your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other sensitive data.

Children's Privacy

Because Bill Tipper collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date. Given the nature of the app, changes are unlikely.
