How it works

Each round, a sequence of tiles lights up across a glowing grid. Your job is to replay it — perfectly, from memory. The sequences grow longer. The grid grows taller. The margin for error shrinks.

Choose your difficulty. Then survive it.

Three ways to play

Easy

Longer flashes, short sequences. Good for warming up your neurons.

Normal

The intended experience. Sharp eyes, sharper memory.

Hard

Brutal pacing. For the ones who think they're ready.
