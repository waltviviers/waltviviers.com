Loki & Potato

Two best friends in a cage.

A pair of white rats live in your browser. Check on them in the morning. They're usually in the hammock.

Play in your browser Visit the girls

Who are they?

Loki is all white — the escape artist, the instigator, the one who got into everything first.

Potato is white with light brown markings — deceptively named, because she was more rocket than couch, always a step behind Loki in whatever daft thing they were doing together.

They were real rats. They were best friends. This is a small way of keeping them around.

What's in the cage

The hammock

Top floor. This is where they sleep — piled on top of each other, the way rats do when they trust you completely.

The bedding

Soft grass on the bottom floor. They dig in it, rearrange it, and occasionally just sit in it for no reason.

The bottle & bowl

Refill them in the morning. Nothing bad happens if you forget — they just look at you with very patient eyes.
