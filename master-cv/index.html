Career Overview

Walt Viviers is a South African fine artist and Creative Director with over twenty years of professional experience spanning fine art, graphic design, brand identity, web design, and digital marketing.

His creative career began in graphic design and art direction, building a portfolio of work for nationally and internationally recognised brands across retail, hospitality, and sport. Throughout this period, he has led campaigns and digital strategies that have delivered significant commercial results for clients including Kaizer Chiefs, Alpha Industries, Sebago, Superga, and Kappa.

In parallel with his design career, Walt has maintained an active fine art practice in acrylic and mixed media. He returned to painting in 2020 and has worked consistently since, with works held in private collections across South Africa and abroad. He is represented on Saatchi Art.
